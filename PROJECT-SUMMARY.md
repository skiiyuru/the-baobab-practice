## 🎨 Design Implementation

### Brand Colors (Applied Throughout)

- **Growth Green (#6F8A6D)** - Section dividers, sub-headings, accents
- **Resilience Clay (#C46A3A)** - CTA buttons, highlights
- **Earth Brown (#4A3728)** - Primary text, headings
- **Warm Sand (#F7F4EF)** - Background colors
- **Supporting Colors** - Additional brand palette shades

### Typography

- Headline font for major headings
- Body font for content
- Consistent sizing hierarchy:
  - H1: 3xl-4xl
  - H2: 2xl-3xl
  - H3: xl-2xl
  - Body: base (16px)
- No contractions - all full words

### Visual Elements

1. **Hero Image:** "The Resilient Baobab" - majestic baobab at golden sunset

- Tablet: Adjusted 2-column grids
- Mobile: Single column, hamburger menu
- All touch-friendly and accessible
