✍️ Typography
Headline Font Options
Use one consistently:
• Playfair Display
• Lora
• Libre Baskerville
These communicate credibility and thoughtful leadership.
________________________________________
Body Font Options
Choose one:
• Inter
• Open Sans
• Source Sans Pro
These ensure clarity and modern professionalism.
________________________________________
Typography Rules
•	Headlines: Bold or semi-bold
•	Body text: Regular weight
•	Avoid excessive font mixing
•	Use generous spacing for calm readability
________________________________________
🌳 Logo Style Direction
Logo should reflect:
✔ Stability
✔ Natural strength
✔ Community gathering symbolism
✔ Simplicity
✔ Longevity
Avoid:
✖ Overly literal tree illustrations
✖ Cartoon or decorative imagery
✖ Complex detailing
Preferred styles:
•	Minimal baobab silhouette
•	Abstract trunk or root symbolism
•	Circle or gathering motif
________________________________________
📸 Imagery Style
Use images that feel:
• Natural light
• Genuine human interaction
• Small group collaboration
• Reflection and dialogue
• Diverse leadership environments
Avoid:
• Stock-photo corporate handshake imagery
• Overly staged boardroom shots
• Highly glossy or artificial scenes
